Breakfast Potatoes

Delicious crispy breakfast potato bites

These golden brown potatoes are seasoned to perfection with The spice mix. They are a perfect pair to your eggs, whether fried, scrambled, or poached.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • The Spice Mix
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 Degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Wash your potatoes
  3. Dice your potatoes in half inch cubes
  4. In a large bowl, sprinkle potatoes with the spice mix, oil and salt
  5. Place your potatoes in a baking sheet and bake in the oven
  6. Set a 15 minute timer
  7. After 15 minutes remove the potatoes from the oven and flip them
  8. Put the potatoes in the oven for another 15 minutes
  9. Let the potatoes cool for a few minutes and enjoy!
