Blackened Salmon

Crispy Salmon seasoned to perfection

This recipe will impress your guests. The seasoning is well balanced with the lime and cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Salmon
  • Salt
  • oil
  • Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle and rub the spice mix on both sides of the salmon
  2. Ina pan pour oil
  3. When the oil is hot, place the salmon on the pan
  4. Flip the salmon after 5 minutes
  5. After 5 minutes, remove the salmon, and sprinkle it with sea salt
  6. garnish with cilantro and squeeze a fresh lime
